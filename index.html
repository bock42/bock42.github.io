Andrew Bock, PhD

Scientist/Researcher

Summary

Experienced researcher in the fields of psychology and neuroscience. My work involves developing image analysis software in Matlab to process large MRI data sets, as well as creating and presenting various visual stimuli for use in functional MRI experiments. In addition, I train and mentor graduate students, undergraduates, and research staff members.
